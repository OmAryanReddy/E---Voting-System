ALGORITHM:

STEP1: After opening the html file you will be able to see a webpage with a heading E-VOTING
SYSTEM

STEP2: First you will be asked to enter your name and father name

STEP3: Upon clicking on the input field of the date, calendar will appear and you need to select
current date from it.

STEP4: Next you have to select your gender from options available and multiple options cannot
be selected.

STEP5: Later on you should fill the field voter id and choose a political party for which you want
to vote.

STEP6: And you have to click submit button to submit your vote upon submission you will be
directed to a thank you page
